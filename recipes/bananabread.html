Banana Bread

Description

This is a recipe for banana bread. You'll need to following ingredients and a 9x5 baking dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 large eggs beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Beat brown sugar and butter together in a separate bowl with electric mixer
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas and blend
  5. Stir in banana mixture into flour
  6. Pour into dish
  7. Bake in oven for about 60 mins
  8. Cool for 10 mins then enjoy!
