French Toast

Description

This is a recipe for banana bread. You'll need to following ingredients and a 9x5 baking dish.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bread
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon together
  2. Place bread slices into the mixture one at a time and flip to ensure both sides are covered
  3. Cook on skillet for 2-3 mins
  4. Enjoy!
